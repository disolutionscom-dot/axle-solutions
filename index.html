About Axle Solutions

Metal Fabrication &
Engineering Solutions

Backed by Firdous Steel & Taj Al Mas

Axle Solutions Metal Ind. LLC is a UAE-based fabrication house — backed by the experience of its parent companies, Firdous Steel and Taj Al Mas — delivering precision-engineered stainless steel, mild steel and aluminium solutions for commercial, industrial and hospitality projects. From a single custom counter to a full commercial kitchen fit-out, we build on quality and deliver with pride.

Our Mission

To engineer durable, precise metalwork that supports the UAE's growing construction, industrial and hospitality sectors — on time, every time.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted metal fabrication partner by investing consistently in machinery, skill and quality standards.
